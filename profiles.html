Analytics Jobs

Many companies visit campus offering analytics profiles to students for placements. This is one of the most common roles seen in placement season. The job description for this role or position varies according to companies and their type of work. This role can be seen in different fields ranging from finance, consulting, IT, etc and the work will be different in each case. In finance domain, there will be use of financial tools and calculations. For that the students should have some basic knowledge of financial terms and tools. In the consulting field, the use of analytics is more towards finding business insights and solutions.

Data Science

Data Science field has been gaining a lot of traction lately in which many big organizations and even governments are starting to take interest in. But for a college going student or for someone who is looking to break into data science, this is not that important but what is important is your interest in the field derived straight out of your love for mathematics and problem solving. Because in it’s most simple terms, data science is just applying mathematics techniques on your problem statement. If you love mathematics, data science is something which will give you a lot of new problems everyday which will be solved with help of your mathematical techniques. Be it time series prediction, classification problem, image processing, language processing etc, there is a lot to learn and explore in the field. If all these things interest you one bit, you can earn a fortune working in the field of Data Science while fulfilling your hunger for problem solving. Cheers
