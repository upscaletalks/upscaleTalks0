Analytics Jobs

Many companies visit campus offering analytics profiles to students for placements. This is one of the most common roles seen in placement season. The job description for this role or position varies according to companies and their type of work. This role can be seen in different fields ranging from finance, consulting, IT, etc and the work will be different in each case. In finance domain, there will be use of financial tools and calculations. For that the students should have some basic knowledge of financial terms and tools. In the consulting field, the use of analytics is more towards finding business insights and solutions.

Further scope of analytics profile?

Analytics is a growing field and due to advancement in the IT sector, now most of the firms are using analytics techniques and tools to make better decisions for their firms and to work more efficiently and effectively. If one enters an analytics role as a fresher then there are multiple options to pursue afterwards. You can choose to enter a more specific and technical field - Data science. There is a lot of scope and earning or growth potential in this field. Moreover, you can learn specific tools and advance your career in that. E.g Tableau expert, Data engineer, etc. Also if you are interested more towards business or client facing side, then you can opt for that or can pursue MBA to join consulting firms for their client facing roles

How to prepare for analytics?

There is no mandatory preparation or skills required for an analytics role visiting DTU. But strategic preparation can make life much easier.

  • Make sure your gpa is not low. Anywhere above 8 is considered to be a decent gpa
  • Make yourself comfortable with some of the basic analytics skills e.g sql, python, excel, tableau, statistics, data analytics. Stepwise approach for learning data analytics:
    • Basics of statistics
    • SQL queries and python syntax
    • Python libraries ( Numpy, Matplotlib, Pandas, Seaborn)
    • Data analysis techniques
    • Tableau
    • ML
  • Do relevant projects to showcase these skills in your resume and to get hands-on experience.
  • Have internships in similar fields where you have worked on projects using similar tools.
  • Having a well structured and detailed resume is very important.
