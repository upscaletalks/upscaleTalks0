All About Upscale

UpScale is a consortium which aims to provide its learning community to learn, grow, adapt and succeed in today’s competitive environment. The team brings forward several people who have gone through rigorous interviews and skill development procedures that have helped them grow as a person

Upscale hosts 30 young and enthusiastic undergraduate students who have been placed at stellar firms and budding startups. They are willing to share their finite learnings with junior undergraduate students in order to help them gain and develop a handy skill-set, aiding them in various situations be it a placement interview or a career decision altogether.

We hope to provide our students an enriching experience, leaving them with positive changes and a broader outlook towards their career!
